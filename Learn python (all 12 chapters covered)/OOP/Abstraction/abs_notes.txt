Key Takeways:
hame abstraction ki ye batein yaad rakhi h ki -

i. Abstraction unnecesssary details ko hide rakhta h aur jo structure h uski complexity kafi aasan bana deta h taki user usko bhot easily use kr paye 

ii. Abstract classes cannot be instanciated wo sirf as a blueprint act krti h 

iii. Child classes must define actual classes.
